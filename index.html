Who is N.W.A?


N.W.A., was an American hip hop group from Compton, California, and is widely regarded as the pioneer group in the formation of the gangsta rap musical subgenre.

Early Years

Ice Cube met last Saturday with his fellow N.W.A. in a surprise performance during the Californian Coachella festival. It was a promise he had made in January, and it marks the culmination of a special year for the line-up, first with the massive success of the Straight Outta Compton biopic, and then with his induction into the Rock & Roll Hall of Fame, just barely. a few days ago, by the hand of Kendrick Lamar. It was the 5th hip hop line-up to enter a Hall normally exclusive to more traditional pop, rock and soul legends.
